About Me

Why I Started the Channel

The reason I started my channel was to easily share and store the montages and memes I would make of clips while gaming with my friends. Many of the views are my friends rewatching the video, and a few videos have over 100 views. Growing the channel was never a priority as, again, it was mainly to store the videos themselves. Videos take a lot of hard drive space so moving them onto youtube helped save storage while keeping them accessible made Youtube an appealing platform.

What Game is Being Recorded?

The game featured in every montage is Rainbow6 Siege by Ubisoft. It is a 5 vs 5 team based shooter game. We have all been playing the game for years and had tons of funny moments and regreted not recording them, so I found out about Nividia Shadowplay as an easy way to record and started making the videos. There have been thoughts of also recording other games, but the reason I haven't is talked about later. The clips featured are from many people's perspective, but mainly mine with the others weaved in here and there.

How come the last Video Was a Year Ago?

Even though I have a lot of clips saved and could make a few videos, I mainly haven't due to college. Working on my degree has taken a lot of time and effort, and I spend my freetime mostly playing the games instead of editing videos of them now. There may be more videos, but for now they are mostly up to stagnant until I get more free time.

I did enjoy making videos, but I am not sure how long this hiatus will be. Until then, enjoy the rest of the videos!
