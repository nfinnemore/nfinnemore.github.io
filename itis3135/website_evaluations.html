Website Evaluations

Arngren

The website for an electronics company to buy their products

  • Contrast - Names are the same font color and size as descriptions, but are bolded leading to little contrast.
  • Repitition - There is very little consistency between font color usage and it seems random.
  • Alignment - Items are randomly sorted and their info boxes go over and under other items with seemingly zero alignment.
  • Proximity - Nothing is sorted near other relevant items and there are many text breaks.

Apple

Apples main website

  • Contrast - All the images and text pop on the white background.
  • Repitition - Color scheme of white and black is repeated on all pages with navigation bar always on the same spot.
  • Alignment - Everything is organised the same way with topics being evenly focused in the center as you scroll.
  • Proximity - Not a lot of info jammed into the page, everything is spaced out.
